Sony Entertainment Network

Content Integration

My Role: UX Designer
Deliverables: Sitemap, wireframes, functional specifications

Overview

This project started out modest in scope. I was contracted to redesign the Sony Entertainment Network landing screen to better showcase Sony's online content, and to efficiently funnel vistors to their content of choice.

Approximately one third of the way into the project, the assignment expanded considerably. I was asked to submit a design integrating Sony's games, video, and music stores as well as account management and tech support sections.

Summary

I'm a user experience designer and front-end developer with a background in information technology (systems and network administration, and end-user support). I combine a user-centered design approach with strong technical chops. I’ve worked as the sole UX designer on various projects and am also a great collaborator.

Skills

Site maps, wireframes, user research, usability testing, personas, use cases, competitive analysis, card sorting, interaction design, information architecture, HTML, CSS, jQuery, prototyping, mobile design, responsive design

Clients

Sony Entertainment Network; Science Inc.: Gumroad; Imagistic: Santa Barbara Botanic Garden; Petrol Advertising: Nexon, Turtle Beach; Marjorie Kaye UX Design, JLR Interactive, PCH Scuba
